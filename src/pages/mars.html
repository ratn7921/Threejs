🪐 Mars: The Red Planet

Formation & Geology

Mars formed ~4.6 billion years ago. Its surface features include giant volcanoes (Olympus Mons), canyons (Valles Marineris), and polar ice caps.

Atmosphere & Climate

Thin CO₂ atmosphere, average temperature −63 °C, frequent dust storms, evidence of ancient liquid water.

Exploration & Habitability

Visited by orbiters, landers, and rovers (Sojourner, Curiosity, Perseverance). Potential past microbial life under investigation.
