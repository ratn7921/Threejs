☄ Jupiter: The Gas Giant

Formation & Composition

Formed ~4.5 billion years ago. Mostly hydrogen & helium; core may be rocky; famous Great Red Spot storm.

Moons & Rings

Over 90 known moons (Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto) and faint ring system of dust.

Exploration

Visited by Pioneer, Voyager, Galileo, Juno missions—continues to reveal deep atmosphere and magnetosphere data.
